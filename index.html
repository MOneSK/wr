📖 Concept
The mechanism sentence is what separates Band 6 from Band 7. After you give a reason (WHY), you explain the exact process or chain of events (HOW). This shows deeper thinking and sophisticated analysis. Many students skip this — don't.
📐 Templates — Click to copy
Study what makes a sentence weak vs strong. Identify the exact gap.
✏️ Practice Task
Write a mechanism sentence for this context:

Topic: "Online learning improves access to education."
Reason: "This is because students can access courses from anywhere in the world."

→ Now write the HOW mechanism sentence explaining the exact chain of events.

🔁 REVIEW · MECHANISM SENTENCE
Context: "Governments should invest in public transport."
Reason: "This is because private car usage contributes significantly to urban pollution."

Task: Complete the mechanism sentence:
Specifically, when more commuters switch to buses and trains, _______
🔁 REVIEW · TOPIC SENTENCE
Essay prompt: "Some people believe that technology has made life more complicated. Do you agree?"

Task: Write a topic sentence for a paragraph arguing in favour of technology simplifying life.
🔁 REVIEW · THESIS STATEMENT
Prompt: "Governments should ban fast food advertising. To what extent do you agree?"

Task: Write a strong, specific thesis statement (one sentence).
